About Me

I am a self taught Designer / Maker. It has been a long process to get to where I am now. It all started about 8 years ago when I had an idea for a project that involved the need for a lathe. After picking up a cheap one at harbor freight I become hooked with the process. Lots of time, in the shop and on Youtube, and lots of cash later I have finally reached a point were I can be proud to say, 'I made that.' I am always striving to work with new material. That exploration is what drives me to make work, while simultaneously making new ideas take a while to get off the ground. I am twenty-eight years old. I grew up kicking around in the dirt in Oldham County, KY. I have always been an outdoors men one way or another, constantly playing in the woods, riding bikes, and getting myself into all sorts of adventures. I have always been into making something with my hands, and its that which drives me most into my endeavor.
